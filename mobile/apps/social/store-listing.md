## iOS App Store

### Subtitle (30 characters max)
Safe Social Network for Kids

### Promotional Text (170 characters max)
A social network built for kids 5-15 with age-appropriate controls, parent-approved contacts, and content moderation on every post.

### Description (4000 characters max)
Bhapi Social is a safe social network designed specifically for children ages 5 to 15. Every feature is built with safety first -- parents approve contacts, content is moderated before posting, and age-appropriate controls adjust automatically.

SAFE BY DESIGN
Unlike adult social networks adapted for kids, Bhapi Social was built from the ground up for child safety. Pre-publish content moderation screens every post from users under 13 before anyone can see it. Rapid takedown protects teens 13-15 with post-publish moderation under 60 seconds.

PARENT-APPROVED CONTACTS
Children can only connect with contacts approved by their parent or guardian. Every contact request triggers a notification to the parent via the Bhapi Safety app, where they can review the requester's profile before approving or denying.

AGE-APPROPRIATE EXPERIENCE
Three age tiers automatically adjust what children can see and do:
- Ages 5-9: Simplified interface, curated hashtags, no direct messages, all content pre-moderated
- Ages 10-12: Expanded features, image sharing, messaging with approved contacts, pre-moderated posts
- Ages 13-15: Fuller social experience with post-publish moderation, configurable privacy, and gradual independence

SHARE AND CREATE
Kids can share posts with text and images, like and comment on friends' posts, follow hashtags they care about, and express themselves in a safe environment. Media uploads are scanned for safety before publishing.

REAL-TIME MESSAGING
Chat with approved contacts in real time. Typing indicators, read receipts, and media sharing keep conversations fun. All messages are monitored for safety with the same moderation pipeline as posts.

FAMILY CONNECTED
Bhapi Social works together with the Bhapi Safety app. Parents get visibility into social activity, contact requests, and content flags -- all without reading every message. Children build healthy digital habits in a supervised environment.

NO ADS, NO TRACKING
Bhapi Social contains no advertising, no third-party tracking, and no data collection beyond what is needed for the service. Built for COPPA, GDPR, and LGPD compliance.

Requires a parent or guardian with an active Bhapi subscription. Visit bhapi.ai for details.
