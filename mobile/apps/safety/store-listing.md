### Description (4000 characters max)
Bhapi Safety gives parents and guardians visibility into their children's AI interactions -- without reading every conversation.

MONITOR AI USAGE ACROSS PLATFORMS
See activity across ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, Claude, Grok, Character.AI, Replika, Pi, Perplexity, and Poe. The Bhapi browser extension captures interactions and flags safety concerns automatically.

REAL-TIME SAFETY ALERTS
Receive push notifications when our AI detects risky content including inappropriate topics, emotional dependency patterns, personal information sharing, or deepfake-related activity. Alerts are categorized by severity so you know what needs immediate attention.

CONVERSATION SUMMARIES
Get plain-language summaries of your child's AI conversations without reading every word. Understand what topics they are exploring and how AI tools respond.

FAMILY GROUPS
Manage safety settings for your entire family. Add up to 5 members, set age-appropriate controls, and customize alert preferences per child.

AGE-APPROPRIATE CONTROLS
Three age tiers (5-9, 10-12, 13-15) automatically adjust monitoring sensitivity, content filtering, and feature availability. Bedtime mode and time budgets help manage screen time.

SCHOOL AND CLUB SUPPORT
School administrators and club managers can monitor AI usage across their organizations with per-seat pricing, class management, and safeguarding reports.

COMPLIANCE YOU CAN TRUST
Built for COPPA, GDPR, and LGPD compliance. Parental consent management, encrypted data storage, and transparent AI safety scoring.

Requires a Bhapi subscription. Plans start at $9.99/month for families. Visit bhapi.ai for details.
