# Bhapi Safety — Release Notes

## v4.0.0 (2026-03-24)

### What's New

**Unified Parent Dashboard**
A redesigned home screen consolidates all family safety insights in one place — activity summaries, risk alerts, and quick actions are now accessible without navigating through multiple screens.

**Screen Time Management with Age-Tier Limits**
Set daily and weekly screen time budgets per child. Limits automatically adjust based on age tier (under 13, teen, young adult) and enforce bedtime mode on a configurable schedule.

**Location Tracking with Geofences**
Create named safe zones (home, school, a friend's house) and receive instant push notifications when a child arrives at or departs from any zone. Background location runs only when geofences are active.

**Creative Tools Review**
Parents can now review AI-generated content from the Social app directly in the Safety dashboard before it is visible to contacts, with one-tap approve or flag actions.

**Cross-App Onboarding**
A unified onboarding flow covers both Bhapi Safety and Bhapi Social setup in a single session — family profile, child accounts, contact approval, and screen time preferences are configured together.

**6 Language Support**
The full app experience is now available in English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, and Japanese, including all notifications and parental consent flows.

### Bug Fixes & Improvements

- Panic button response time reduced to under 2 seconds on all tested devices
- Alert badge counts now clear reliably after review
- Improved accessibility contrast ratios across all screens (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- Push notification delivery improved for low-connectivity environments
