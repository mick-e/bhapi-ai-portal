## Long Description (4000 chars max)

**The social network designed for kids — safe by design, fun by choice.**

Bhapi Social is a safe, moderated social network for children aged 5–15. Every post is reviewed before it appears. Every friend request needs parental approval. Every interaction happens in an environment built for kids, not adults.

### Why Bhapi Social?

Kids want to connect with friends. But mainstream social networks expose them to bullying, inappropriate content, and strangers. Bhapi Social gives kids the social experience they crave in an environment parents can trust.

**Parent-Approved Connections**
No strangers here. Every friend or contact request your child sends or receives must be approved by a parent first. Your child's social circle stays exactly who you expect it to be.

**Safe by Design — Moderated Content**
Every post, photo, and message is screened by our AI moderation system before it goes live. Harmful content never reaches your child's feed. For children under 13, every piece of content is reviewed before publishing (under-13 pre-publish screening).

**Creative Expression**
Kids can share photos, write posts, use fun stickers and filters, and express their creativity — all within a safe, age-appropriate environment. Creative tools are tailored to each age group.

**AI Literacy Built In**
Bhapi Social includes interactive AI literacy lessons woven into the experience. Kids learn how AI works, how to spot misinformation, and how to use technology responsibly — while having fun.

**Real-Time Safety Status**
Children see their own "safety score" — a friendly, age-appropriate indicator of how safely they're using the platform. It encourages positive behaviour without shame or punishment.

**Age-Appropriate Design**
The app's look, feel, and features adapt to your child's age tier (5–9, 10–12, 13–15). Younger children see simpler interfaces; older teens get more features as they demonstrate responsible use.

**Parental Visibility**
Parents using the companion Bhapi Safety app get visibility into social activity — alert notifications, weekly summaries, and the ability to pause social access instantly.

**Privacy & Data Protection**
Bhapi Social collects only what's needed to run the service. No advertising. No data selling. No tracking for commercial purposes. COPPA and GDPR compliant.

**No Ads. Ever.**
Bhapi Social is funded by families, not advertisers. Your child will never see targeted ads or sponsored content designed to exploit their attention.

### Who is Bhapi Social for?
- Children aged 5–15 who want a safe space to connect with friends
- Parents who want oversight without removing all social opportunities
- Schools that want to encourage safe digital citizenship

**Download Bhapi Social and give your child the social experience they deserve — safe, fun, and always parent-approved.**

*Bhapi Social is best used alongside Bhapi Safety — the companion parent monitoring app that gives you full oversight.*
