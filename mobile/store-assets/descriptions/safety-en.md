## Long Description (4000 chars max)

**Keep your kids safe in the age of AI.**

Bhapi Safety gives parents complete visibility into how their children use AI tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, Claude, and more — with real-time alerts, smart content controls, and detailed weekly reports.

### Why Bhapi Safety?

AI assistants are now part of your child's daily life — for homework, creativity, and socialising. But most parents have no idea what their kids are actually discussing with AI. Bhapi Safety changes that.

**Real-Time Monitoring**
Get instant alerts the moment your child encounters concerning content — harmful advice, inappropriate topics, emotional manipulation, or academic dishonesty. Alerts are graded by severity so you know exactly when to act.

**Full Activity Visibility**
See a complete timeline of your child's AI conversations across all major platforms. Understand which tools they use most, how much time they spend, and what topics they explore.

**Smart Content Blocking**
Set daily time budgets, enforce bedtime mode, and block specific AI platforms — automatically. Rules you set on your phone apply instantly across your child's devices.

**Family Agreements**
Create digital family agreements that set clear expectations about AI use. Both parents and children sign together, so everyone understands the rules.

**Weekly Safety Reports**
Get a plain-English weekly digest with highlights, trends, and personalised recommendations. No jargon — just the information you need to guide your family.

**Privacy First**
Bhapi Safety never reads your child's conversation content in a way that violates their privacy. Our AI analyses patterns and risk signals — not full transcripts — and all data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

**School & Club Ready**
Bhapi works alongside school monitoring tools. School admins can share safety reports directly with parents, creating a joined-up safety net.

**COPPA & GDPR Compliant**
Built from the ground up for regulatory compliance. Bhapi Safety meets COPPA 2026 requirements, GDPR (EU), and LGPD (Brazil) — you stay in control of your family's data.

### Who is Bhapi Safety for?
- Parents of children aged 5–15 who use AI tools
- Families who want to set healthy AI boundaries
- Schools and clubs looking to extend safety monitoring to the home

**Download Bhapi Safety today and take back control of your family's AI journey.**
