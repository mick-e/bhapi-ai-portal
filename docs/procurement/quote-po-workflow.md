## 1. Overview

This document describes the procurement workflow for schools and districts purchasing the Bhapi AI Safety Monitoring Platform. Bhapi supports standard public-sector procurement processes including formal quotes, purchase orders (POs), and Net 30 invoicing.

## 3. Procurement Workflow

### Step 1: Initial Contact

- School or district contacts **sales@bhapi.ai** or submits an inquiry via bhapi.ai.
- Bhapi sales representative schedules a discovery call to understand needs (student count, grade levels, AI platforms in use, compliance requirements).

### Step 2: Pilot (Optional)

- Bhapi provides a 14-day free trial for evaluation.
- No purchase order or contract required for the pilot.
- Pilot includes full platform access for up to 50 students.
- Bhapi provides onboarding support during the pilot period.

### Step 3: Formal Quote

- Bhapi sends a formal quote including:
  - Per-seat pricing based on student count.
  - Contract term (typically 1 year, aligned to school year).
  - Included services (platform access, browser extension, support, training).
  - Applicable discounts (multi-year, district-wide, Title IV-A funding eligible).
- Quote is valid for 60 days.
- Quote is provided in PDF format suitable for board approval.

### Step 4: SDPA and Compliance Review

- District reviews and signs the [Student Data Privacy Agreement (SDPA)](sdpa-template.md).
- Bhapi provides the [Vendor Security Questionnaire](vendor-security-questionnaire.md) and [FERPA Compliance](../compliance/ferpa-compliance.md) documentation.
- Bhapi cooperates with district legal, IT, and privacy reviews.

### Step 5: Purchase Order

- District issues a Purchase Order (PO) referencing the Bhapi quote number.
- PO should be sent to **billing@bhapi.ai** or mailed to the address on the quote.
- Bhapi confirms receipt of PO within 2 business days.

### Step 6: Account Setup

- Upon PO receipt, Bhapi creates the school/district account within 3 business days.
- Bhapi provides:
  - School admin account credentials.
  - Onboarding guide and training schedule.
  - Browser extension deployment instructions (Chrome/Firefox/Safari).
  - Integration setup for Clever, ClassLink, Google Workspace, or Entra (as applicable).

### Step 7: Invoicing

- Bhapi issues an invoice referencing the PO number.
- **Payment terms:** Net 30 from invoice date.
- **Payment methods:** ACH, wire transfer, check, or credit card.
- Invoices are sent to the billing contact specified on the PO.

### Step 8: Annual Renewal

- Bhapi sends a renewal notice 60 days before contract expiration.
- Renewal quote reflects updated student count and any pricing changes.
- District has 30 days to confirm renewal, request changes, or provide non-renewal notice.
- If the district does not renew, data deletion procedures begin per the SDPA (Section 7).

## 4. Funding Eligibility

Bhapi may qualify for funding under:

- **Title IV-A** (Student Support and Academic Enrichment) — AI safety monitoring supports safe and healthy students.
- **E-Rate** (where applicable to internet safety monitoring).
- **State technology grants** — varies by state.

Bhapi can provide documentation supporting funding applications upon request.
