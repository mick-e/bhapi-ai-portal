## 2. Coverage Details

### 2.1 Data Breach Response

Covers costs associated with responding to a data breach, including:

- Forensic investigation.
- Legal counsel.
- Notification to affected individuals (as required by state and federal law).
- Credit monitoring services for affected individuals.
- Public relations / crisis management.
- Call center services.

### 2.2 Technology Errors & Omissions

Covers claims arising from:

- Failure to perform technology services as promised.
- Software errors or defects causing customer harm.
- Unintentional infringement of intellectual property rights.

### 2.3 Network Security Liability

Covers third-party claims arising from:

- Unauthorized access to or use of computer systems.
- Transmission of malicious code.
- Denial-of-service attacks.
- Failure to protect confidential information.

### 2.4 Privacy Liability

Covers claims arising from:

- Violations of privacy laws (COPPA, FERPA, GDPR, state laws).
- Unauthorized collection, use, or disclosure of personal information.
- Failure to comply with privacy policies.

### 2.5 Regulatory Defense & Penalties

Covers costs of:

- Defending against regulatory investigations (FTC, state AGs, EU DPAs).
- Fines and penalties where insurable by law.
