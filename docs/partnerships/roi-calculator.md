## Notes for Presenters

When presenting this ROI analysis to a school board or administrator:

1. **Lead with compliance risk**, not cost savings — compliance requirements are non-negotiable; cost savings are a bonus
2. **Use the incident cost estimate conservatively** — even one FERPA or COPPA inquiry can cost $10k+ in legal review
3. **Emphasize staff relief** — IT directors and safeguarding leads are overloaded; Bhapi automation is a retention and wellbeing tool for staff too
4. **Reference Ohio HB 96** (or your state's equivalent AI in education mandate) — regulatory requirements provide urgency
5. **Offer a pilot** — Bhapi offers a 14-day trial and pilot pricing for districts evaluating before full rollout
