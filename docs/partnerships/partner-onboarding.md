# Bhapi Partner Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the Bhapi Channel Partnership Program. This guide covers everything you need to start earning recurring revenue by bringing AI safety to schools and communities.

---

## Program Overview

Bhapi is an AI safety and governance platform for families and K-12 schools. We help parents and school administrators monitor and manage children's AI tool usage — across ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, Claude, Grok, and more — while maintaining full COPPA, GDPR, and FERPA compliance.

As a partner, you earn a recurring commission for every family or school you onboard. Our customers stay long-term because Bhapi is operationally embedded in their day-to-day safety workflows — meaning your commissions compound over time.

| Tier | Approval Required | Commission | Best For |

## Onboarding Steps

### Step 1 — Apply

**Referral Partners:** No application needed. Email [partners@bhapi.ai](mailto:partners@bhapi.ai) with your name, company, and website. You will receive your unique referral link within 1 business day.

**Reseller and Strategic Partners:** Email [partners@bhapi.ai](mailto:partners@bhapi.ai) with:
- Company name, website, and primary contact
- Target market (school districts, EdTech, MSP, etc.)
- Estimated annual deal volume (if known)
- Brief description of your existing customer base

### Step 2 — Approval Review

The Bhapi partnerships team reviews Reseller applications within 5 business days. We check:
- Alignment with our target markets (schools, families, youth organizations)
- No active conflict of interest with competing AI safety tools
- Basic compliance posture (FERPA/COPPA awareness expected for school-facing partners)

You will receive an approval email with your Partner Agreement to sign via DocuSign.

### Step 3 — Onboarding Session

All approved partners complete a 60-minute onboarding session (video call with your partner success manager) covering:
- Product walkthrough (key screens, value propositions)
- Sales motion: who to target, common objections, deal timeline
- Co-branded materials walkthrough
- Partner dashboard orientation
- Support channels and escalation path

Schedule your session at: [calendly.com/bhapi-partners](https://calendly.com/bhapi-partners)

### Step 4 — Go Live

After the onboarding session you receive:
- Your unique referral/reseller link (tracked with 60-day cookie window)
- Access to the Partner Dashboard (real-time earnings, conversions, customer status)
- Co-branded one-pager (PDF, editable)
- ROI Calculator for school decision-makers
- Deployment Guide (Chrome extension, SSO, SIS sync)
- COPPA/GDPR compliance documentation package
- Partner newsletter subscription (product updates, new features, sales tips)

## Commission Model

### Rate and Duration

- Referral Partners earn **10% of MRR** for the life of the customer
- Reseller Partners earn **15% of MRR** for the life of the customer
- Strategic Partners have a negotiated rate that may include one-time bonuses

"Life of the customer" means you continue earning for every month the customer remains subscribed — including upgrades. There is no expiry, no cap, and no clawback unless a customer was referred fraudulently.

### Attribution

- Referral links use a 60-day cookie window
- If a prospect visits via your link, leaves, and purchases within 60 days, the sale is still attributed to you
- For Reseller partners placing orders directly, attribution is always 100% guaranteed

### Payment

- Commissions are paid on the **1st of each month** for earnings from the prior calendar month
- Payment is via **Wise bank transfer** (free for most recipients; Wise fees apply in some jurisdictions)
- Minimum payout threshold: **$50 USD**. Balances below $50 roll to the next month automatically
- You must have a verified Wise account linked in your Partner Dashboard before receiving your first payment
- A commission statement (PDF) is emailed on the 1st of each month alongside the transfer

1. Partner Slack channel / partner email — for general questions
2. Partner Success Manager — for deal support, compliance questions

- Handled by Bhapi directly (standard) — customers email support@bhapi.ai

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I partner if I also sell competing products?**
Reseller and Strategic partners must not actively sell a competing AI safety monitoring platform to the same customer base. Referral partners have no exclusivity requirement.

**Is there a partner certification or exam?**
Not currently. The 60-minute onboarding session is the only requirement. We may introduce optional certification for Strategic partners in future.

**What happens if a customer I referred churns and later returns?**
If the customer re-subscribes within 12 months, the commission re-activates to your account automatically. After 12 months, the customer is no longer attributed.

**Can I refer international schools?**
Yes. Bhapi serves schools in the US, UK, EU, Canada, and Australia. Commission structure is the same regardless of customer geography.

**How do I see my earnings?**
Log in to the Partner Dashboard at bhapi.ai/partner-dashboard using your partner credentials. Earnings are updated within 24 hours of a subscription payment clearing.
